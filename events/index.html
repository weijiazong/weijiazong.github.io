EliteForsk Travel Grant

The foyer of the Opera, Copenhagen, Denmark • 28.2.2019

In March 2019 I received the EliteForsk travel grant. The Elite Research Travel Grant is DKK 200,000 and helps very talented PhD students to perform longer-term studies in some of the best research environments in the world. The prize was handed to him by her Royal Highness Crown Princess Mary of Denmark and the Danish Minister of Science, Technology, Information, and Higher Education Tommy Ahlers.
